X-We want to believe

This game was made for the TV Game Jam 19 (01/02/2019 to 15/02/2019). This is a short interactive fiction based on X-files where you make simple decisions in order to advance the story.

Team: Pierre-Antoine Cinquin (Game Design and Storytelling), Maelle Le Montagner (3D models and textures), Anna Loeff (Text redaction and correction), and Joan Sol Roo (Game Design and Implementation). Amazon Amy was used for text-to-speech.

You can play the game HERE.

The game received very positive reviews.
