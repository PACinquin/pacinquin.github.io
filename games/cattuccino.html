Cattuccino

This game was made for the Coffe Jam 18 (21/07/2018 to 28/07/2018). You play as an alien cat stucked in a coffee machine, trying to serve the beverages ordered. Spoiler: it's nearly impossible and will undoubtedly end in a big mess.

Team: Pierre-Antoine Cinquin (Game Design, Sound, Music), Leo Cousin (Art) and Joan Sol Roo (Game Design, Implementation).

You can play the game HERE.

As a lot of time, we started with a totally different idea and changed direction mid-jam. Thus, it is more a new-retro experience than a game.
