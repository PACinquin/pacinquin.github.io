Godship

A Metroidvania game that gets harder the more violent you are. Developed using the fantasy 8-bit console PICO-8. This game was initially made for the Ludum Dare 41 but we missed the deadline. It then slowly expand over 4 months, driven by the challenge of implementing physics system from scratch respecting the constraints of hte platform.
