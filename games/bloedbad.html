BloedBad

This game was made for the Ludum Dare 44 under the theme "Your life is currency". Retro Metroidvania where Health is used to pay for weapons and to open doors (in order to make future runs easier). The game was made with Unity3D and assets were made with Aseprite.

Team: Pierre-Antoine Cinquin (Art), Anna Loeff (Art), Thibault Laine (Game Design, Implementation, Level Design), Erwan Normand (Implementation, Sound), and Joan Sol Roo (Game Design, Art, Implementation).

You can play the game HERE.

For this one I wanted to focus on the graphic part, and thus engage in Pixel Art. It was quite fun and satisfying. Working collaboratively with Anna who had a degree in art was also really positive as we could learn different things from each other.

The game received a important amount of good feedback, and ranked in top 5-20% depending on the category.
